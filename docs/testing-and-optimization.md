# Testing And Optimization

## Test Philosophy

The system tests one variable at a time. More content is not a valid fix unless the message, offer, audience, and landing page are already working.

## Variables

Test one of these per experiment:

- hook
- offer
- format
- persona
- channel
- landing-page headline
- CTA

## Primary KPI Rules

Scale when there is business signal:

- at least one booked call
- at least one qualified lead
- measurable CRM pipeline value
- repeated target-buyer comments and saves

Fix landing page when:

- clicks are high
- leads are low
- page conversion is weak

Fix audience or offer when:

- impressions are high
- engagement is low
- comments come from non-buyers
- leads are unqualified

Stop when:

- no useful signal after 14 days
- no commercial value after 30 days

## Experiment Record Minimum Fields

- hypothesis
- campaign
- persona
- variable
- content id
- review window
- decision
- notes

## Content Quality Checklist

Before approval:

- Hook is specific and non-generic.
- Proof source exists.
- CTA maps to a landing page or CRM offer.
- UTM is present.
- No unsupported statistics.
- No guaranteed outcome claim.
- No personal/customer data without consent.
- AI-generated media is tracked.
- Instagram hashtags are 3 to 5 focused tags.
